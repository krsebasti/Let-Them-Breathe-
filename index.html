Introduction

Jail overcrowding is still a big problem in the Philippines. Many jails are already full. Some even go beyond their limit. Because of this, inmates live in places that are crowded and uncomfortable.
This happens because legal cases take a long time to finish. Many people are still waiting for their trial. They stay in jail longer even without a final decision yet. That is why the system finds it hard to manage all the cases.
In order to get a better jail system, our project will focus more on providing Pro-Bono Lawyers in order to lessen the processing inmates in jail also to avoid overcrowding in jail facilities.
By providing Pro-Bono Lawyers to inmates they will not be in jail for a long time since they will be defended by the provided Pro-Bono Lawyers instead of waiting for a Paid Lawyer, by doing this their time in the jail will decrease and the jail population will also decrease.

Mission:

The project aims to develop an ICT-based Pro Bono Legal Integration Platform that connects indigent Persons Deprived of Liberty (PDLs) with volunteer lawyers and law students to ensure timely and accessible legal representation. Through the use of smart-matching technology, real-time virtual consultations, and digital case tracking, the platform seeks to reduce delays in legal proceedings, prevent prolonged detention, and help decongest overcrowded jails in the Philippines. It is committed to promoting a fair, efficient, and humane justice system by ensuring that every individual—regardless of financial capacity—has equal access to legal assistance and due process.

Vision:

Our objective is for the Philippines to have a fair and compassionate legal systems in which jail are not overcrowded and all prisoners are treated with respect and dignity, Our Objective is to provide Pro-Bono Lawyers to lessen the processing inmates, ensuring that they receive timely and proper legal assistance. Through this, we aim to reduce delays in the justice system, prevent prolonged detention, and promote equal access to justice for all individuals regardless of their financial status.
